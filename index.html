No plastic waste: no problem!

Borebo is a waste upcycling project spearheaded by a passionate group of students, with a primary focus on tackling the ever-growing issue of plastic waste.

Inspired by the powerful slogan "No plastic waste: no problem!" we aim to spark a transformative shift in the community's perception of recyclable materials. By upcycling waste into high-value products, Borebo strives
to contribute to environmental protection while fostering creativity and promoting the art of handicraft.

Frequently asked questions

What materials are currently being recycled and sold by this project?

Answer: We have currently launched one product called Sweet Box. The flowers in this box are recycled from plastic bottles. In the future, we will sell other products recycled from environmentally harmful materials such as bottles, plastic cans, aluminum cans, cardboard, toilet paper rolls, plastic bottle caps, plastic spoons, damaged tires, etc.

What are the benefits of selling recyclable materials?

Answer: Selling recyclable materials helps reduce waste, conserves resources, and can generate income. It also supports environmental sustainability by promoting recycling and reducing the need for raw materials.
